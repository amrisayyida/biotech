About Meridian

A Singular Focus on Oncology Innovation

Founded on the conviction that understanding tumor biology at its deepest level is the path to transformative medicines, Meridian Oncology combines world-class translational science with disciplined clinical development.

Our approach integrates proprietary target discovery, rational drug design, and biomarker-driven patient selection to develop therapies addressing the most critical unmet needs in oncology. We focus on treatment resistance and the tumor microenvironment.

Our Science

Three Platforms Driving Discovery

Integrated science from target identification through clinical proof-of-concept.

Precision Target Discovery

Proprietary computational and functional genomics platform identifying novel targets by mapping the genetic dependencies of treatment-resistant tumors at single-cell resolution.

Rational Drug Design

Structure-guided medicinal chemistry combining AI-driven molecular modeling with high-throughput screening to optimize candidates for potency, selectivity, and pharmacokinetics.

Biomarker-Driven Development

Integrated translational strategy embedding companion diagnostics from the earliest stages to identify patients most likely to benefit from our therapies.
